Privacy Policy

Effective Date: November 2025

Confession Manager is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data the app handles and how it is stored.

1. Data Storage and Security

All data you enter in Confession Manager (such as names, notes, confessions, and reminders) is stored securely on your device using Apple’s Core Data framework. Your information never leaves your device unless you choose to back it up via iCloud.

2. iCloud Sync (Optional)

If you enable iCloud, your app data will be securely stored and synchronized via Apple’s iCloud service using CloudKit. This data is encrypted and associated only with your Apple ID. Confession Manager does not have direct access to your iCloud account.

3. No Data Collection

Confession Manager does not collect, transmit, or share any personal or usage data. We do not use analytics, tracking, or third-party advertising frameworks that send data off your device.

4. In-App Purchases

Payments for premium features (such as removing ads) are processed securely through Apple’s App Store. Confession Manager does not handle or store any payment information.

5. Ads and Premium Upgrade

The free version may display non-personalized ads. No personal data is shared with advertisers. Upgrading to Premium removes all ads.

6. Your Rights

You can delete all app data at any time by removing the app from your device. If you use iCloud, deleting the app data in iCloud will remove all synced content.

7. Contact

If you have questions or concerns about this policy, please contact:

support@paulhenien.com

We take privacy seriously and continuously work to maintain user trust through transparency and secure design.
